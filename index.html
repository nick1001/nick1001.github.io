Crocker's Rules

I declare Crocker's Rules and invite you to speak your mind without consideration of politeness, convention, or political correctness. Communication may be optimized for information exchange, and not for being nice. Being that CR is a one way road, all return communications will remain in standard customary format.

COMz

  • Prefer COMs over text/voice/video/real-time? Make it known.
  • I do not do well with reflective/reactive conversation.
  • Real-time Communication - I prefer Telegram and voice
  • Communication time sensitive? Send 'ping' to see if I'm available. The traditional response is 'pong'.
